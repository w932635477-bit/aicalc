Frequently Asked Questions

›How often is the pricing data updated?

We verify pricing from official provider pages regularly. Each model shows its last-verified date. LLM pricing changes frequently — always double-check the provider's official page before making purchase decisions.

›What does "Cached $/1M" mean?

Some providers offer discounted pricing when input tokens are served from a cache. Anthropic, OpenAI, and Google all charge 10% of base input for cached reads. DeepSeek charges just 2%. This can dramatically reduce costs for repeated prompts.

›Why is Groq missing cached pricing?

Groq (Llama 4 Maverick) uses a high-throughput inference architecture that does not currently offer separate cached token pricing. All requests are charged at the standard input rate.

›Which model is cheapest for production use?

For high-volume tasks, DeepSeek V4 Flash ($0.14/1M input) and Gemini 2.5 Flash-Lite ($0.10/1M input) are the most affordable. For quality-sensitive work, Claude 3.5 Haiku ($0.80/1M) or GPT-5.4 Mini ($0.75/1M) offer a good balance.
